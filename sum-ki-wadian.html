Sum ki Wadian Tour

3 Days & 2 Nights | Pakistan

Tour Overview

Explore the breathtaking beauty of Sum ki Wadian. This tour is specially designed for families and adventure lovers who want to escape into nature and enjoy amazing landscapes, lush green mountains, and cold freshwater streams.

What's Included

  • Luxury Transport
  • Standard Hotel Stay
  • 2 Times Meals
  • Professional Tour Guide
  • Photography
  • First Aid Kit

Itinerary (سفر کا شیڈول)

Day 1: Departure & Arrival

Departure from Lahore/Islamabad, short stopovers on the way, arrival at the destination, hotel check-in, and dinner.

Day 2: Exploration Day

Full day exploration of the beautiful valleys, streams, local sightseeing, and a bonfire night with music.

Day 3: Return Back

Breakfast at the hotel, departure back to the home city, arrival late in the evening.
