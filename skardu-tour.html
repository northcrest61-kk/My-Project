Skardu Expedition & Adventure

7 Days & 6 Nights | Baltistan

Tour Overview

Skardu is a land of giants, cold deserts, and emerald lakes. This 7-day adventure takes you to Lower Kachura (Shangrila Lake), Upper Kachura Lake, the vast Cold Desert of Shigar, Khaplu Valley, and the high-altitude plains of Deosai National Park. Perfect for true explorers!

What's Included

  • Dedicated Transport & Fuel
  • 6 Nights Accommodation
  • 4x4 Jeeps for Deosai Plains
  • Daily Breakfast & Dinner
  • Cold Desert & Shigar Tour
  • Professional Tour Manager

Itinerary (سفر کا شیڈول)

Day 1: Departure for Chilas

Early morning drive via Motorway and KKH. Long drive through mountains, night stay at Chilas hotel.

Day 2: Skardu Highway & Shangrila Resort

Drive along the Indus River on Skardu Road. Visit Shangrila Resort (Lower Kachura Lake) and Upper Kachura Lake. Night stay in Skardu.

Day 3: Shigar Valley & Cold Desert

Explore Shigar Fort, Sarfaranga Cold Desert (world's highest cold desert), and Blind Lake. Night stay in Skardu/Shigar.

Day 4: Deosai National Park Adventure

Take 4x4 jeeps to explore Deosai Plains, Sadpara Lake, and Sheosar Lake. Look out for the Himalayan Brown Bear! Night stay in Skardu.

Day 5: Mantoka Waterfall & Khaplu

Visit the majestic Mantoka Waterfall and take a cultural trip to the historic Khaplu Palace. Night stay in Skardu.

Day 6: Drive Back to Chilas

Pack up and travel back through the winding Skardu highway towards Chilas for the last night stay.

Day 7: Arrival Back Home

Final stretch of travel back to Islamabad/Lahore. Arrival late at night.
