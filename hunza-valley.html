Hunza Valley Premium Tour

5 Days & 4 Nights | Gilgit Baltistan

Tour Overview

Experience the magical Hunza Valley, often called heaven on earth. From the historical Altit & Baltit forts to the breathtaking views of Rakaposhi and the crystal-clear Attabad Lake, this premium tour offers the perfect blend of culture, history, and natural luxury.

What's Included

  • Premium 4x4 or Saloon Coasters
  • Deluxe Hotel Accommodation
  • Daily Breakfast & Dinner
  • Attabad Lake Boating Tickets
  • Tour Guide & Jeep Expenses
  • Entry Fees for Forts

Itinerary (سفر کا شیڈول)

Day 1: Journey via Karakoram Highway

Departure from early morning. Travel through Hazara Motorway and Karakoram Highway. Short stop at Chilas / Jaglot for a night stay.

Day 2: Arrival in Hunza & Sightseeing

Stop at Rakaposhi Viewpoint. Move to Karimabad, Hunza. Visit Altit Fort, Baltit Fort, and enjoy the sunset at Duiker (Eagle's Nest).

Day 3: Attabad Lake & Khunjerab Pass

Visit the stunning Attabad Lake for boating. Cross Passu Cones, and travel up to the Pakistan-China Border (Khunjerab Pass). Night stay in Hunza.

Day 4: Naran or Besham Transfer

Breakfast in Hunza, travel back towards Chilas or Naran (depending on Babusar Top accessibility) for the final night stay.

Day 5: Return to Home City

Travel back to Islamabad/Lahore. Reach safely with unforgettable memories of the majestic north.
